# Product

**register:** brand

## Users
Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Arjay Delos Reyes as a software engineering candidate.

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio that converts recruiter visits into interview conversations by demonstrating technical depth and professional quality.

## Brand
Professional, clean, minimal. Confident without being boastful. The work speaks for itself.

## Tone
Direct and clear. No buzzwords, no filler. Every word earns its place.

## Anti-references
- Generic dark blue dev portfolios
- Animated progress bars for skills
- Glassmorphism cards
- Hero sections with large metric numbers and gradient accents
